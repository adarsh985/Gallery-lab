Salman Khan

Bollywood Superstar • Philanthropist • Bhai of the Nation

Biography

Salman Khan, born on December 27, 1965, is one of the biggest stars in Indian cinema. He made his debut in 1988 with Biwi Ho To Aisi, and became a household name after Maine Pyar Kiya. Known for his charming screen presence, action-packed roles, and charitable work, Salman is a true icon of Bollywood.

Achievements & Highlights

  • Blockbuster movies like Bajrangi Bhaijaan, Sultan, Wanted, and Tiger series.
  • Host of the popular TV show Bigg Boss for multiple seasons.
  • Runs the non-profit charity Being Human Foundation.
  • Known for supporting new talent and helping people in the industry.
  • Massive fan following across the globe, known as “Bhai” by fans.

Famous Quote

"Do whatever you want to do man, but don’t trouble your mother."
